## Slide 5 — Data flow
**Sensor → Core → Crypto → Alert.**
- Sensors detect motion, pressure, PCB breaches
- VEGA RISC-V core is the secure computing heart
- Crypto module verifies firmware integrity at boot
- LoRa sends tamper alerts up to 15 km
- Power management for low-power secure operation

## Slide 6 — Game-changing differentiators
1. First Indian tamper-aware node on the open-source VEGA RISC-V platform
2. Multi-layer tamper detection (motion + pressure + PCB breach, not just one)
3. Ultra-fast crypto erase (<500 ms vs 2-5 s for foreign devices)
4. Long-range alerts via LoRa (15 km) and BLE 5.2
5. Indigenous & open architecture for defense and critical IoT
6. **90% cost reduction**: TAP-RISC ~₹10,000 vs imported HSMs ₹1,00,000+
